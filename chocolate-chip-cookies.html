Chocolate chip cookies

If you are a sweettooth then this recipe is just for you!

These chocolate chip cookies are so good that you are not going leave any crumbles left

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Steps

  1. Preheat the grill to 350 degrees F (medium-high).
  2. Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.
  3. Use your hands to form 4 hamburguer patties 3/4 inches thick
  4. Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned
  5. Enjoy your delicious chocolate chip cookies
