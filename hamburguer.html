Hamburguer

Who doesn't enjoy eating a tasty and flavorful hamburguer?

I can tell you that I love hamburguers with lettuce, sliced tomatoes, grilled cheese, and a fat piece of grilled meat

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds 80% lean 20% fat ground beef or ground chuck
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 4 iceberg lettuce leaves, rinsed and crisped
  • 1 firm-ripe tomato, cored and thinly sliced

Steps

  1. Preheat the grill to 375 degrees F (medium-high).
  2. Divide the beef into fourths
  3. Use your hands to form 4 hamburguer patties 3/4 inches thick
  4. Place the burgers on the grill. Cook 4-5 minutes on the first side. Flip the burgers over and cook an additional 4-5 minutes, until the burgers have reached the desired doneness
  5. Lay a slice of cheese on each burger patty about 1 minute before taking the burgers off the grill, so the cheese has a chance to melt
  6. Serve the burgers on hamburger buns with the sliced lettuce and the sliced tomatoes
